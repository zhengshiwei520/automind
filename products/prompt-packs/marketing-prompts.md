## How to Use This Prompt Pack

### Getting the Best Results

1. **Be specific with context.** Every prompt below is designed to be copy-pasted directly into ChatGPT. However, for maximum results, append your specific details (brand name, product URL, target audience, etc.) in brackets or parentheses after the prompt.

2. **Iterate and refine.** Treat the first output as a draft. Use follow-up prompts like: *"Make this more conversational"*, *"Shorten this to 100 words"*, *"Add more data points"*, or *"Write this for a C-suite executive audience."*

3. **Feed it your data.** ChatGPT performs best when you give it context. Paste in your analytics data, customer emails, competitor URLs, or SEO reports before or alongside these prompts.

4. **Use personas.** For many prompts, start with: *"You are an expert [X] with 15 years of experience. Your task is to..."* — this dramatically improves output quality.

5. **Always fact-check.** AI can hallucinate statistics, dates, and citations. Verify any hard numbers before publishing.

### Pro Tips for Maximum ROI

- **Save your favorites.** After testing, bookmark the 15–20 prompts that work best for your specific business.
- **Build prompt chains.** Combine prompts from different categories to build complete campaigns (e.g., SEO → Content Strategy → Copywriting → Email Marketing).
- **Create templates.** Once you get a great output, save it as a template and reuse it with minor tweaks.
- **Batch your work.** Run 5–10 prompts in one session to maintain consistency across your marketing assets.

## Category 1: Social Media Content Creation (Prompts 1–25)

### 1 — LinkedIn Thought Leadership Post
**Prompt:** "Write a LinkedIn thought leadership post from the perspective of a seasoned marketing director. The post should discuss one counterintuitive lesson about [social media engagement in 2026]. Start with a bold hook in the first line, include a personal anecdote in the middle, and end with a question to drive comments. Keep it under 1,200 characters. Use short paragraphs and no jargon."

### 2 — Instagram Carousel Script
**Prompt:** "Create a 10-slide Instagram carousel script about [the top 5 mistakes brands make with AI-generated content]. Brand voice: confident, educational, slightly provocative."

### 3 — Twitter/X Thread
**Prompt:** "Write a 12-tweet Twitter/X thread that breaks down [one complex marketing concept — e.g., 'how attribution modeling actually works']. ### 4 — TikTok Script — Educational
